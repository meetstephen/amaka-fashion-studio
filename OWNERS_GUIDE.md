# Amaka Fashion Atelier — Owner's Guide

A simple, non-technical guide to managing your website from your phone or computer.
No coding needed — everything is done through your **Admin Panel**.

---

## 1. How to open your Admin Panel

You have two private ways in (there is **no public button** — visitors can never find it):

**Option A — The secret tap (easiest on your phone)**
1. Open your website.
2. Scroll to the very bottom of any page.
3. Tap the copyright line (`© Amaka Fashion Atelier...`) **5 times quickly** (within 3 seconds).
4. You'll be taken to the login page. You'll feel a tiny gold flash on each tap.

**Option B — Type the address**
- Go to `your-website-address/admin/login`

**Your password:** the one you chose during setup. Keep it private. If you ever forget it, your developer can reset it.

> Tip: After 5 wrong password attempts, login locks for 15 minutes. This protects you from strangers guessing.

---

## 2. The Dashboard

After logging in you'll see your dashboard, grouped into three areas:

- **Operations** — Orders/Inquiries, Bookings, Customer Accounts, WhatsApp Templates
- **Content** — Images, Site Copy, Lookbook, Featured, Announcements, Testimonials
- **Insights** — Analytics (who's visiting your site)

To leave the admin and view your live site, tap **Return to Site** (the green bar at the bottom on mobile).

---

## 3. Managing photos (the most important part)

### Add new photos
1. Go to **Images** (or **Lookbook** for your gallery).
2. Tap **Upload images** to choose photos from your phone gallery (you can pick several at once), **or** tap **Take photo / Camera** to snap one right then.
3. Wait for the "Uploading..." spinner to finish. Your photo appears immediately.

### Replace a photo
- On any photo, tap the **"Tap to replace"** badge in the corner, then choose the new photo.

### Rename or delete a photo
- **Images:** tap **Rename** to change its name/category, or **Delete** to remove it.
- **Lookbook:** edit the title/caption directly under each photo, or tap **Delete**.

> Everything you upload shows on your public website right away.

---

## 4. The homepage Featured image

1. Go to **Featured**.
2. Upload your chosen photo and edit the **Title** and **Subtitle**.
3. Tap **Save Changes**.
4. To remove the photo, tap **Remove Current Image**.

This is the large banner near the top of your homepage — use your best seasonal piece here.

---

## 5. Editing your words (Site Copy)

- Go to **Site Copy** to change text like your tagline, story, and section headings.
- Tap **Save** when done.

---

## 6. Testimonials (client reviews)

- Go to **Testimonials** to add what happy clients say (name, city, their words, a star rating).
- The reviews appear on your homepage. **If you add none, that section stays hidden** — so it never looks empty.

---

## 7. Announcements bar

- Go to **Announcements** to show a thin banner at the top of the site (e.g. "Festive orders close Dec 1").
- Toggle it on/off and edit the text any time.

---

## 8. Seeing your business activity

- **Orders / Inquiries** — every customer request, with a status you can update (New → In Progress → Quoted → Won).
- **Bookings** — schedule fittings, consultations, and deliveries.
- **Customer Accounts** — client profiles and saved measurements.
- **Analytics** — how many people visited, which pages they viewed, and where they came from.

> These all start **empty and clean** — they fill up as real customers use your site.

---

## 9. How customers reach you

- **WhatsApp** — every "Order", "Book", and "Enquire" button opens a WhatsApp chat to your number (+234 913 127 2407). Keep WhatsApp handy!
- **Dapper (the chatbot)** — your AI style assistant answers visitors' questions any time and guides them to WhatsApp.
- **Measurements page** — customers can send their body measurements online; they land in your **Customer Accounts**.

---

## 10. Everyday tips

- **Use real, well-lit photos.** Good photos sell garments — they are the single biggest thing that makes the site shine.
- **Reply to WhatsApp quickly.** The whole site funnels serious buyers there.
- **Add a few testimonials** once you have happy clients — they build trust.
- **Log out** when using a shared device (tap **Logout** in the admin).
